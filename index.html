Blackpink

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group signed under YG Entertainment located in 397-5 Hapjeong-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul, South Korea. Blackpink currently consists of four members; Jisoo, Jennie, Rose and Lisa. Blackpnk debuted on August 8, 2016 with their single album Square One: Boombayah being their debut song followed by Whistle and many other hits.
